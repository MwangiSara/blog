Etema Park

Africa's Wildlife Park

Etema Park was started in 1890 by 4 renowned persons. Beatrice Mugoiri, Sarah Wanjiru, Brian Liyosi and James Mogusu were the founders of etema part. Etema park was previously just a normal playground for kids until the founders decided to spice it up by adding a zoo, camp, and basically everything that etema park is. Etema parked was closed for about an year before they reopened with at least 10 lions and 13elephants.
